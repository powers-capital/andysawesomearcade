Fighting Games

  • Tekken is a fighting game series featuring a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique fighting styles and special moves, and players use a combination of buttons and joystick movements to execute attacks and combos. The series is known for its fast-paced action and deep mechanics, as well as its rich and complex storylines.
  • Mortal Kombat quickly became known for its intense and graphic violence, as well as its deep fighting mechanics. The games feature a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique fighting styles and special moves, and players use a combination of buttons and joystick movements to execute attacks and combos. The series is also known for its signature "Fatality" finishing moves, allowing players to finish off their opponents in a brutal and spectacular way.
  • Street Fighter is a classic fighting game series featuring diverse characters, iconic special moves and deep mechanics, it is a pioneer in the fighting game genre and continue to be popular among players and tournaments.

Driving Games

  • Cruising Exotica is a racing game developed by Midway Games in 2000, features a unique racing experience set in tropical locations, where players race on jet skis and speed boats, avoiding obstacles and collecting power-ups. It was a popular arcade game and was well-received by critics. 
  • San Francisco Rush is a racing game developed by Atari Games, released in arcades in 1996, features high-speed action racing through the streets of San Francisco, with realistic physics and multiple play modes, it was well-received by critics and considered a classic in the arcade racing genre.
  • Arctic Thunder Arctic Thunder is an arcade racing game developed by Midway Games in 2001. Players race on snowmobiles through various snowy environments, including mountains and canyons, while avoiding obstacles and collecting power-ups. The game features a variety of tracks and game modes, including a split-screen multiplayer mode. Players can also customize their snowmobiles with different parts and decals. Arctic Thunder was well received by critics and was popular in arcades.
